Welcome to Scala Labs

If you are looking for a great, hands-on way to learn Scala, then this is the place for you. This site will host a (hopefully soon) huge collection of hands-on labs that will teach you the ins and outs of this great programming language.

Current Status

The idea for this site came out of a small in-company workshop to teach our colleagues something about Scala. This means that are starting with 4 intermediate labs and one advanced Lift web application. In the coming months we are going to be hosting more public hands-on sessions for which we will be creating more labs. Our idea for the future is that people will start contributing labs to this site and create a fun, one-stop community for learning Scala.
